Undergraduate Admissions

Begin your academic journey with us at Barbara University. We provide a diverse curriculum and a supportive learning environment. With opportunities for research, leadership, and international programs, our undergraduates thrive in an innovative setting.

Graduate Programs

Elevate your career with our graduate programs. Our advanced degrees provide in-depth knowledge, real-world applications, and one-on-one mentorship from world-class faculty.

Research Opportunities

Engage in meaningful research in science, arts, technology, and humanities. Barbara University offers funded research opportunities for both graduates and undergraduates.

Areas of Study

At Barbara University, academic excellence meets real-world impact. We offer programs across disciplines that prepare students to be leaders, innovators, and problem solvers.

  • Engineering & Technology – Cutting-edge labs and faculty innovators
  • Business & Economics – Practical experience and international connections
  • Humanities & Arts – Explore creativity, culture, and communication
  • Sciences – Biology, Chemistry, Physics and Interdisciplinary Programs
  • Education – Train to be future-ready teachers and curriculum leaders

Board Statement

The University Board of Barbara University proudly reaffirms our dedication to the institution’s founding mission: to cultivate an environment of academic excellence, inclusive community, and transformational learning.

As we move forward, our focus is clear: enhancing the student experience, expanding accessibility through financial aid and scholarships, and investing in faculty and facilities that drive discovery and opportunity.

On behalf of the entire board, we thank our students, faculty, alumni, and partners for their ongoing support and commitment to our shared values.
