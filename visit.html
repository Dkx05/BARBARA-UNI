Visit Barbara University

Come for the campus tour, stay for the free cookies (and maybe an education)

See For Yourself Why We're Worth the Student Loans

Pictures are great, but there's nothing like experiencing our campus in person (the smell of old books and fresh coffee is hard to capture digitally). Whether you're a prospective student, proud parent, or just really into college architecture, we'd love to show you around.

Ways to Visit

Campus Tours

Our student guides will show you all the highlights, from the library to the best nap spots. Tours last about 90 minutes and include:

  • Academic buildings
  • Residence halls
  • Dining facilities
  • Secret student spots
Schedule a Tour

Open Houses

Experience Barbara U like a real student. Open houses include:

  • Faculty meet-and-greets
  • Sample classes
  • Student panels
  • Free swag
Register for Open House

Virtual Visits

Can’t make it in person? Join us virtually with:

  • 360° tours
  • Live Q&As
  • Digital viewbooks
  • Virtual dorm tours
